Tweetfollow is a simple web app built to do one thing, let you know when you lose twitter followers. You can just give us your twitter name and the e-mail associated with the account, and we'll send you e-mails once a day with lists of the users that have unfollowed you.

Don't worry we don't keep your e-mail or sell it.
